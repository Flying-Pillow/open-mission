Open Mission is the operator surface for the Open Mission daemon.

Its panes are projections over daemon-owned state:

- **Workflow Control**: command, stage, task, and selection control
- **Agent Execution**: live Agent execution
- **Briefing Room**: artifacts and focused Mission documents

Open Mission may cache local surface preferences such as layout and selection. Domain state, workflow legality, Mission runtime data, and Entity behavior belong to the daemon.
